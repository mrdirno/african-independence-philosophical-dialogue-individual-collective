## Entry Points for Contributors

**Start anywhere. Fork freely. No permission needed.**

### Story Entry Points

- Write a chapter where Kwame and Ama listen to the artifact's recording for the first time, revealing a pivotal but forgotten speech from the independence era.
- Create a monologue from the perspective of Kofi Mensah in his 'containment,' explaining why he chose Kwame as the artifact's keeper.
- Write a scene where Ama uses her diagrammatic logic to decode a hidden pattern or message in the wax print cloth itself.
- Develop a confrontation at the Philosophy and Chop Bar between Kwame and one of the 'other men' who followed Yaw, turning a philosophical debate into a tense, real-world negotiation.

### Technical Entry Points

- Build an interactive 'Argument Map' website that visualizes the novel's core philosophical debate, allowing users to trace the dialogue between individual and collective positions through quoted text.
- Create an AI-assisted 'Sankofa Archive' tool that lets users upload descriptions of personal or historical artifacts and generates a short story about the 'unfinished argument' that object embodies.
